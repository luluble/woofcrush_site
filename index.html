About Us

WoofCrush is here for all of your pet product needs.

WoofCrush, a division of luluble, specializes in creating high-quality pet grooming products by incorporating cutting-edge technologies and proprietary knowledge from the hair and skin care industries. Our products are sustainable and derived from natural sources, ensuring both efficacy and environmental responsibility.

We partner with our clients to offer not just products but a complete experience. From initial concept to troubleshooting, our team provides technical expertise at every stage. Our formulations meet stringent standards, ensuring safety and quality for your pets. Whether you're a groomer, vet, or pet lover looking to start a pet care business, WoofCrush is your go-to partner for innovative pet care solutions.
